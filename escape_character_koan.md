**USAGE 1: ESCAPABLE CHARACTERS**

>__without backslash__
>>*literal asterisks*

>__with backslash__
> \*literal asterisks\*

***
**USAGE 2: PLUS**
> __without backslash__
> + Plus Symbol

> __with backslash__
> \+ Plus Symbol 


***
**TABLE OF CHARACTERS**
| CHARACTER | DEFINITION |
| ---- | ---- |
|\ | backslash |
|() | parentheses|
|` | backtick|
|# | hash mark|
* | asterisk
+ | plus sign
_ | underscore
- | minus sign (hyphen)
{} | curly braces
. | dot
[] |square brackets
! |exclamation mark


> ATTENTION!:
> ---
> ALWAYS PUT __\\__ SYMBOL BEFORE __ESCAPE CHARACTER__ IF YOU WANT TO AVOID MISTAKE
